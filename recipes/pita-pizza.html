Pita Pizza

Description

This is a recipe to make small pizzas with pita bread.

Ingredients

  • Pita bread
  • Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Place the pita bread on a cutting board or flat surface
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly across the pita bread
  4. Sprinkle cheese over the sauce
  5. Place pepperonis on top of the cheese
  6. Bake in the oven for 6-8 minutes depending on desired crunchiness
  7. Remove from oven and let them cool off
  8. Enjoy!
